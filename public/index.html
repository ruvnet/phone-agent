Phone Agent

Welcome to Phone Agent

This service helps you schedule and manage phone calls with AI agents.

Simply forward your calendar invites to our service, and we'll handle the scheduling automatically.

How It Works

  1. Forward your calendar invite to schedule@your-domain.com
  2. Our system will extract the meeting details
  3. An AI agent will be scheduled to make the call at the specified time
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email with the details

API Endpoints

  • /api/webhook - Receives email notifications
  • /api/schedule - Handles scheduling requests
